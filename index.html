About Our School

Katiadi Government Pilot Model High School stands as a beacon of educational excellence in Kishoreganj since 1941. For over eight decades, we have been committed to nurturing young minds and shaping future leaders through quality education and holistic development.

As one of the most prestigious government secondary schools in the region, we offer comprehensive education in Science, Humanities, and Business Studies streams, ensuring that every student finds their path to success.
